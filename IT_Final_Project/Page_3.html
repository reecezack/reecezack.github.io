Life at Mizzou

I came to The University of Missouri in the Winter of 2020 after spending the first semester of my freshman year playing baseball at Missouri Valley College. I loved Mizzou as soon as I stepped foot on campus. It was a big change compared to the school I came from because of the size of the school. It is now almost 2023 and I am only one semester away from graduating. I have learned a lot and am a way different person now than I was that second semester of my freshman year when I first moved into the dorms at Mizzou.
